Popular Destinations

Discover the natural wonders and cultural treasures of North Sumatra. From the breathtaking views of Lake Toba to wildlife adventures in the jungle, these destinations offer unforgettable experiences for every traveler.

  • Samosir

    Toba Caldera Park

    A scenic park offering panoramic views of Lake Toba, volcanic landscapes, and unique Batak culture experiences.

  • Langkat

    Bukit Lawang

    A jungle village famous for its orangutan sanctuary and eco-tourism activities along the Bohorok River.

  • Karo

    Berastagi

    A cool mountain town known for its volcanoes, fresh produce markets, and views of Mount Sinabung and Mount Sibayak.

  • Karo

    Sipiso-piso Waterfall

    A breathtaking 120-meter waterfall with a dramatic plunge into a gorge, offering incredible photo opportunities.

  • Langkat

    Tangkahan

    A hidden jungle paradise offering elephant bathing experiences and peaceful river tubing in lush surroundings.

  • Simalungun

    Haranggaol

    A tranquil lakeside village surrounded by mountains, perfect for fishing, boating, and enjoying nature.

  • Sibolga

    Mursala

    A stunning coastal area known for its offshore Mursala Island waterfall that flows directly into the sea.

  • Simalungun

    Parapat

    A vibrant lakeside town serving as the main gateway to Samosir Island, with hotels, restaurants, and lake cruises.

  • Sibolga

    Kalimatung Island

    A small tropical island near Sibolga with white sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life.
